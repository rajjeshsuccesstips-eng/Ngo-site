About Us

History: Jan Shakti Seva Samiti was founded on 26 January 2000, registered under Societies Registration Act 1860. We are committed to creating equitable social systems inspired by Gandhian principles of peace and brotherhood and foundered by Damodar das.

Objectives:

  • Empowering women through skill development, education, and awareness
  • Educating children from marginalized communities
  • Conducting health awareness and preventive healthcare camps
  • Promoting sustainable agriculture and environmental care
  • Supporting rural development with community-based planning

Founder’s Message: “We believe in the power of collective action to bring lasting social change. Together, let us build healthier, educated, and empowered communities.”

Our Work / Programs

Education

Literacy drives, free coaching centers, school material distribution for underprivileged children.

Social Justice

Awareness campaigns, legal aid, and advocacy to support marginalized communities’ rights.

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups, vocational training, health and hygiene workshops for women.

Health

Regular health check-up camps, sanitation drives, and substance abuse awareness programs.

Environment

Tree plantation drives, waste management campaigns, and promotion of organic farming.
